# Drop your existing instruction files here

Place any `.md` files you already use to instruct Claude into this folder.

EW will read them automatically when building out your skill profiles.

Examples of what to drop here:
- Your current Claude style guide
- A brand voice document
- Any writing rules you've already written out
- Previous persona or tone instructions

No renaming or formatting required — drop and go.
